Overview

MISSION BRIEF

A busy founder was losing context every morning — scattered across Gmail, Calendar, and Notion. I built a cron-triggered OpenClaw agent that synthesises all sources at 7AM and delivers a structured WhatsApp briefing. Completely autonomous — the agent initiates without any prompts.

Problem & Solution

DIAGNOSIS & FIX

⚠ The Problem

Every morning: 15 min checking calendar, 20 min reading emails, 10 min scanning Notion tasks. Meetings missed. Context lost. 45 minutes wasted before real work started.

✓ The Solution

Cron job fires at 7AM IST. Agent pulls today's Calendar events, unread Gmail threads, Notion task board, and runs web searches on mentioned names/companies. Synthesises into a structured WhatsApp briefing: meetings, key emails, priority tasks, and attendee briefing notes.
