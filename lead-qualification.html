Overview

MISSION BRIEF

A real estate agency was spending 15+ hours per week manually qualifying inbound leads through Telegram. Response times averaged 45 minutes, causing high drop-off rates. I built a fully autonomous OpenClaw agent that handles the entire qualification pipeline end-to-end — from first message to CRM entry.

Problem & Solution

DIAGNOSIS & FIX

⚠ The Problem

Agents were manually responding to every Telegram inquiry — slow, inconsistent, and burning 15+ hours/week. High-value leads were going cold while agents were stuck on low-intent inquiries.

✓ The Solution

Deployed an OpenClaw agent on the client's VPS monitoring Telegram 24/7. On new inquiry: sends qualifying questions, scores the lead on budget/timeline/intent, logs to HubSpot, and fires a WhatsApp alert to the sales team with a full brief — all within 60 seconds.
