Overview

MISSION BRIEF

A founder was drowning in 80+ emails per day. Repetitive support requests, vendor replies, scheduling — all requiring manual attention. I built an OpenClaw agent connected to Gmail via OAuth2 that autonomously handles 85% of daily volume, freeing 6 hours every single day.

Problem & Solution

DIAGNOSIS & FIX

⚠ The Problem

80+ emails daily consuming 6 hours of inbox time. Repetitive support questions and vendor replies were destroying productive hours. Slow responses were frustrating customers.

✓ The Solution

OpenClaw agent deployed on VPS, connected to Gmail via OAuth2. Reads every incoming email, classifies by intent and urgency, auto-responds using a RAG knowledge base, logs all actions to PostgreSQL, and sends WhatsApp alerts only for edge cases requiring human review.
